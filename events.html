PAST IEEE EVENTS

"Image Processing Workshop using LabVIEW" was conducted on September 6th and 7th, 2017. Led by Mr. Nishant Singh, Senior Technical Consultant at ProTreX, Mumbai, the workshop delved into advanced image processing techniques. Participants gained hands-on experience applying LabVIEW under expert guidance. The event aimed to enhance practical skills in image processing using this powerful software.

"Exploring Raspberry Pi" workshop took place on September 13th and 14th, 2017. Led by Mr. Rajeshwaran, Director of Realtime Technosoft, Chennai, the session focused on practical aspects of Raspberry Pi technology. Attendees gained hands-on experience and valuable insights into its applications. The workshop aimed to enhance understanding and skills in working with Raspberry Pi.

A guest lecture on cloud computing was presented on September 7th, 2017, by Mr. Karthikeyan, Group Leader at NIIT, Chennai. The lecture provided insights into the evolving landscape of cloud technology, with Mr. Karthikeyan sharing expertise from his role at NIIT. Attendees had the opportunity to gain valuable knowledge about cloud computing during this informative session.

A guest lecture on the Internet of Things (IoT) was presented on September 8th, 2017, by Mrs. Sakthi, Group Leader at NIIT, Chennai. Mrs. Sakthi shared valuable insights into the dynamic field of IoT, drawing on her expertise as a leader at NIIT. The lecture provided attendees with a comprehensive understanding of IoT technologies and their applications.

A Creative Design Workshop was held on October 12th and 13th, 2017. Mr. Mithoon, CEO of Empower Edutech, addressed students, while hands-on sessions were conducted by Mr. Shubham Melvin Felix and Mr. Surya Narayanan. The workshop aimed to enhance design skills through practical insights and creativity under expert guidance.

The IEEE Awareness Programme was conducted on August 28th, 2017, featuring presentations by Dr. M.S. Godwin Premi, Branch Counsellor, Mr. V. Vedanarayanan, Staff Coordinator, and Executive Committee Members of the IEEE Student Branch. This informative session provided valuable insights into IEEE initiatives, fostering awareness among participants about the organization's goals and opportunities.

The IEEE Student Professional Awareness Programme took place on January 29th and 31st, 2018. This program aimed to enhance students' professional awareness within the IEEE community. Attendees had the opportunity to engage with valuable insights shared during the sessions, contributing to their understanding of professional development within their field.

The IEEE Membership Development Program took place on January 30th and February 1st, 2018. Geared towards promoting IEEE membership, the program emphasized the benefits and contributions to professional development. Participants engaged in learning sessions aimed at fostering a sense of community and collaboration within the global IEEE network.

SILICON 2K18 is the annual Sathyabama Intercollegiate Level IEEE Conference organized by the IEEE Student Branch of Sathyabama Institute of Science and Technology in Chennai. In collaboration with the IEEE Madras Section, the event hosts various technical events and symposiums, fostering knowledge exchange among students and professionals in the field of electrical and electronics engineering.
