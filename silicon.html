SILICON

Sathyabama Intercollegiate Level IEEE Conference (SILICON) is organized every year by IEEE Student Branch of Sathyabama Institute of Science and Technology (Deemed to be University), Chennai in association with Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE) Madras Section. Many technical events are conducted during the symposium. The symposium represents a unique opportunity for experts and beginners to gain insight into the rapidly changing scenario.It thus provides a forum for exchange of information and ideas between engineers, scientists, researchers, graduates and post-graduates.
